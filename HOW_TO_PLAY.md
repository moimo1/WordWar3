# 🧟 Word War Z: Official Combat Manual

## 🎯 The Objective
Welcome to **Word War Z**, a fast-paced, competitive multiplayer PvP typing strategy game! Your goal is simple: construct grammatically valid English sentences using randomized words to build powerful attacks, and drop your opponent's HP to 0!

## ⚔️ The Combat Loop
1. **The Word Pool:** Every turn, both players receive a randomized pool of exactly **12 Words**. Each word is physically mapped to a specific *Part of Speech* (Noun, Verb, Adjective, etc.) and carries a random magic *Effect Number* (Damage, Heal, Shield, etc.).
2. **Drafting:** Look at the Word Pool and start tying on your keyboard to string together a sentence. Valid words will snap into your **Sentence Builder** actively at the bottom of the screen.
3. **Submission:** Press `[ENTER]` or `[SPACE]` to lock in a word, and press `[ENTER]` on an empty buffer to lock in the final attack. Once submitted, your keyboard locks until the Server resolves the turn!

## 📜 The Rules of Grammar
To deal damage, your sentence strings must perfectly match one of the server's strictly enforced grammatical **Formulas**. If you string together random disjointed words (e.g., *"The The Shield Attack"*), your turn will completely fizzle into harmless "**Gibberish**", dealing 0 damage!

Here are the most common Valid Attack Formulas you can reliably build:
* **The Basic Strike:** `Verb` (*"Attack!"*) or `Noun + Verb` (*"Knight strikes"*)
* **The Descriptive Strike:** `Article + Noun + Verb` (*"The knight strikes"*)
* **The Advanced Strike:** `Article + Adjective + Noun + Verb` (*"The brave knight strikes"*)
* **The Styled Strike:** `Noun + Verb + Adverb` (*"Knight strikes fiercely"*)
* **The Targeted Strike:** `Noun + Verb + Preposition + Noun` (*"Knight attacks against dragon"*)

## ⚡ Speed & Initiative
Size matters, but so does raw speed! 
When both players submit their sentences, the Server evaluates who physically strikes first based strictly on **Combo Length**:
* **Fast Combos:** The player who built the *shorter* sentence (e.g., 2 words vs 5 words) gets **INITIATIVE** and strikes their opponent first!
* **Heavy Combos:** Longer sentences (e.g., 6 words) pack significantly more damage effects, but you guarantee that the opponent's shorter attack will hit you first. 
* **Simultaneous Strike:** If both players submit the exact same number of words, both attacks land simultaneously!

## 🧪 Magic Effects
Every single Word in your pool visually displays a Number and a specific Outline Color. This represents its underlying physical payload. When a sentence is successfully evaluated, **all effects** embedded within that sentence are triggered simultaneously!

* 🔴 **Red (Damage):** Raw damage that chunks away the opponent's HP.
* 🔵 **Blue (Defense):** Builds a physical Shield block to absorb incoming damage for the active turn.
* 🟢 **Green (Heal):** Restores your missing raw HP.
* 🟠 **Orange (Critical):** Massive combo multipliers that amplify your entire sentence output.
* 🟣 **Violet (Weaken):** Debuffs and breaks down the enemy's structural output.
* 🟡 **Yellow (Amplify):** Ramps up the scaling of your internal stats.

## 🛡️ Pro Tips
* **Don't Overextend:** You are never required to use all 12 words! Sometimes quickly submitting a basic 2-word `Noun + Verb` to guarantee you get Initiative and finish off a low-HP enemy is much smarter than spending 15 seconds typing a massive 6-word masterpiece!
* **Pay Attention to the Colors:** A massive 5-word sentence is totally useless if none of the words you chose actually have any Red Damage numbers attached to them! Build your sentences based on what stats you explicitly need that round!
